About Me

Having worked as a Certified Public Accountant in Washington, I saw the changes that software has and will bring not only to businesses, but to our world in its most fundamental way. I believe that to make the best possible impacts, I have to be in the filed of technology to give it my best.


Joyce Liao moved to the United States with her family in 2002 as permanent residents. Since then, she has obtained her US Citizenship and graduated from University of Washington Business School. She obtained her license as a Washington State CPA in 2014. With the hope to make a greater and meaningful impact to the community, she decided to take software development as her full-time career in 2017. So far, she had approximately one year of project experience in Python and JavaScript, building full-stack web apps utilizing supporting packages such as Django, Flask,PostgreSQL, Docker, j-Query, and Node.js. Her web apps are deployed on AWS, Digital Ocean and Heroku.
